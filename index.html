Service Flow

1. Register

Borrower and Lender, registers on mobile apps.

2. Accept Agreement

Borrower and Lender view term and condition, then accept agreement on mobile apps.

3. Borrow

Borrower start borrow process, then system will be generated contract for Borrower and Lender to sign.

4. Sign digital document

Borrower and Lender sign on contract via mobile apps.

5. Receive money

Borrower receive money via mobile apps.

6. Money transfer back

If due date, then Borrower transfers money with interest back to Lender.
